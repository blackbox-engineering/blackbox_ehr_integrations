Process

Our integrations follow a predictable formula.

  • 1. Kick off

    Lets agree on success

    We work with your internal engineering team to agree on the data format to be handed off into your system. If you do not have an internal data model, we will share our standard best practices model with your team.

  • 2. Access

    Get access to what we need

    We will make sure that your client success team knows what kind of EHR access will require. It is up to your team to identify and get sign off from the Hospital's key stakeholders.

  • 3. Data export

    Two weeks of data extraction.

    We send your engineering team 1 month exports every day as we build out the queries. Your team will have the opportunity to provide feedback on the exported data format during the week. We will agree on the exported format at the end of the week. The second week will be a deep dive where we export your 1 year of data and set up HL7 feeds or nightly batch export jobs.

  • 4. Warranty

    Keep the data coming

    If you purchased an HL7 feed, or nightly batch extracts, we will ensure that the scripts work for 1 month after the integration is complete. If you need updates or fixes to the exports after the month is up, we charge $150 an hour.
